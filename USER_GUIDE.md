## ❓ FAQ

### **General Questions**

**Q: How often are properties updated?**
A: Daily. New properties added regularly, sold properties removed immediately.

**Q: Are prices negotiable?**
A: Yes! Contact the agent to discuss pricing.

**Q: Can I visit the property?**
A: Absolutely! Schedule a viewing through the virtual tour form or contact us directly.

**Q: What does "AI Score" mean?**
A: Our AI rates properties based on location, amenities, price, market trends, and investment potential (1-10 scale).

### **Technical Questions**

**Q: Why isn't the gallery loading?**
A: Check your internet connection. Try refreshing the page. Clear browser cache if issues persist.

**Q: Mortgage calculator shows wrong currency?**
A: Calculator uses property's listed currency. Amounts are accurate for that currency.

**Q: Can I save properties without an account?**
A: Yes! Favorites save to your browser. Create account for cross-device sync (coming soon).

**Q: 360° tour not working?**
A: Ensure JavaScript enabled. Try a different browser. Some corporate networks block iframes.

### **Property Questions**

**Q: What does "Blockchain Verified" mean?**
A: Property ownership and details verified on blockchain. Extra layer of authenticity and trust.

**Q: Are photos recent?**
A: Yes. Properties require recent photos (within 30 days). Date stamps available on request.

**Q: Can foreigners buy property in UAE?**
A: Yes, in designated freehold areas. Our agents can guide you through the process.

**Q: What's included in the price?**
A: Base property price. Additional costs (fees, taxes, registration) discussed during consultation.
